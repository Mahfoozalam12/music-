Features

  • Instruments: Piano, Guitar, Violin, Bass, Synth — each with unique timbre
  • Chords: Click chord buttons or press 1-7 for instant chord playback
  • Arpeggio: Toggle arpeggio mode to play chord notes sequentially
  • Melodies: Select predefined melodies and press Play
  • Volume/Tempo: Adjust in real-time using the sliders

Tips

  • Click keys with mouse or use keyboard
  • Hold multiple keys for polyphonic chords
  • Use Transport controls to start/stop melodies
  • Try different instruments for the same melody
